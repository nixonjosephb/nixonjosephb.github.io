Dewpoint 1.0

Purpose:
Dewpoint is a personal project with funding acquired from WPI Makerbucks program. The purpose and goal of this project other than to learn more about Arduino, 3D printing, and circuitry, was to keep my plants safe and healthy.

Method:
Working with an Arduino Uno I have set up a system to take values from sensors to determine soil moisture levels and then adjust the moisture levels with water pumps.

Future Improvements to be made:
Succulent plants can get sunburnt if facing the same direction towards the sun for too long. Future iterations will include a rotating platform to spin the succulents Other desirable improvements include camera live feed as a backup option to the soil moisture sensor and additional for documenting growing cycle of plants

Water can also be obtained from reclaimation of atmospheric moisture, future iterations will make the project entirely self sustainable
